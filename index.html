Screen Addiction

Screen Time and Kids

Screen time recommendations for children have shifted over the years. The most recent recommendations set these limits:

Up to 6 months old: no screen time 6 months to 2 years old: use screen time only for interactive social play with an adult or to video chat with loved ones

2 to 5 years old: no more than one hour of screen time

School-aged kids: no set time limit, but parents should limit social media use and gaming

Long-term Effects

In children, effects of screen addiction may include: Speech delay Cognitive impairment Difficulty with problem-solving and creative thinking Cyber bullying and exposure to predators Body weight issues and poor bone health due to lack of physical activity, which later in life can add up to heart disease and other health conditions Depression and anxiety
